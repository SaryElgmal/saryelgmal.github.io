Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2025

Thank you for using EasyDM. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using the app, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. However, we use third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase Analytics – to understand usage behavior and improve the app.
  • Google AdMob and AppLovin – to serve ads. These may collect identifiers like your device ID, IP address, or ad interaction data.

2. How We Use Information

The data collected by third-party services is used to:

  • Display relevant ads
  • Monitor app performance
  • Improve user experience

3. Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized ads via:

  • Google Settings on your Android device → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • AppLovin privacy settings (see link below)

4. External Services

EasyDM allows users to open apps like WhatsApp, Telegram, Signal, Viber, and SMS apps to send messages. We do not store or transmit your contact list or phone numbers. All data is handled locally on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

EasyDM is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

6. Compliance with Laws

  • GDPR (EU Users): You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us for requests.
  • CCPA / CPRA (California Residents): You can request disclosure of data collected and request deletion. We do not sell personal data.
  • CalOPPA: This policy is accessible from within the app and our website. We do not track users across third-party websites.

7. Security

We do not store any user data on our servers. All third-party services we use implement their own industry-standard security protocols.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users via app updates or notices if material changes are made.
